Welcome to Rinkal Agarbatti Works

    Established in 1993 ‘Rinkal Agarbatti Works’ is a manufacture of Gugal batti, Loban batti, Masala batti and Incens sticks also. The company has the advantage of preserving the traditional art and science of making Agarbatti.


    All these products are carefully manufactured using finest quality base material and ingredients that are sourced from reputed and certified vendors of the market. Known for their delightful fragrance, even burning, non-toxicity, less smoke emission, and various other distinctive features. The range of our scented incense stick is widely used in homes, temples, aromatherapy centres and various other places for masking bad odour and creating a soothing environment.


    In order to successfully cater to the growing demands of the clients, we have developed a state-of-the-art infrastructure facility, which is fully equipped with all requisite machines and technologies. Systematically constructed with modern architecture, this unit provides a safe and excellent working condition to the professionals.
