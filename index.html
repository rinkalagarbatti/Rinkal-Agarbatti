Rinkal Agarbatti Works

About us

          Established in 1993 ‘Rinkal Agarbatti Works’ is a manufacture of Gugal batti, Loban batti, Masala batti and Incens sticks also. The company has the advantage of preserving the traditional art and science of making Agarbatti.

Product

          Gugal dhoop sticks are highly-fragrant resin that is obtained from the sap of Gugul tree. Known as one of the most fragrant substances available, it has been widely used for centuries to scent up perfumes.
